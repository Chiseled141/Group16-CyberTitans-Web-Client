How Mentor Matching Works

1. Find Your Mentor

Browse mentors above, filter by your target skill, and check their reputation score to find the right fit.

2. Send a Request

Click "Mentor Request" on their profile card. 500 Cyber Coins are deducted from your balance to confirm the booking.

3. Learn & Grow

The mentor reaches out to schedule your 1-on-1 session. After the session, leave a review to update their reputation score.
