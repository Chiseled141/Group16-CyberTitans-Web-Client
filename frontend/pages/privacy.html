Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register as a CyberTitans member, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full name, username, and email address (required for account creation)
  • Phone number (optional, used for club communications)
  • Skills, bio, and experience data (entered by the member for portfolio generation)
  • Activity data: project contributions, workshop attendance, CTF participation, and reputation scores

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for club management and portfolio generation purposes:

  • To create and maintain your member account and verified portfolio
  • To calculate your reputation score and leaderboard ranking
  • To facilitate mentor matching based on skill compatibility
  • To send club-related notifications and activity updates

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to any third parties outside the club.

3. Data Storage & Security

All member data is stored in a secured MySQL database hosted on club infrastructure. Passwords are hashed using BCrypt before storage and are never stored in plain text. Authentication uses JWT tokens with expiry controls.

Access to the database is restricted to authorized administrators. All API communication uses HTTPS in production environments.

4. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your personal data via your Profile page at any time.
  • Correction: You can update your name, email, phone, and bio directly from your Profile page.
  • Deletion: You may request account deletion by contacting a club administrator. Contribution records are retained for club history.

5. Contact

For any privacy-related concerns, contact the CyberTitans admin team via the or reach out to a club administrator directly on the Team page.
