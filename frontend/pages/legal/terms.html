Terms of Service

Effective: March 2026 · IU CyberTitans Club

1. Acceptance of Terms

By creating an account on the CyberTitans platform, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. If you do not agree, you may not access or use the platform. These terms apply to all members, regardless of role (Recruit, Operative, Master, Mentor, Admin).

2. Member Conduct

All members are expected to:

  • Provide accurate information during registration and profile setup
  • Treat all other members, mentors, and administrators with respect
  • Use the platform only for legitimate club activities and learning purposes
  • Not attempt to manipulate, exploit, or circumvent the reputation or coin systems
  • Not share login credentials or impersonate other members

3. Cyber Coins & Tier System

Cyber Coins are a virtual in-platform currency with no monetary value. They cannot be exchanged for real money, transferred between accounts, or refunded. Coins earned through verified contributions are awarded automatically by the system.

Tier upgrades (Operative, Master) are permanent once unlocked and cannot be downgraded by the member. Administrators reserve the right to revoke tier access in cases of misconduct.

4. Mentor Responsibilities

Members with Mentor status agree to:

  • Respond to mentorship requests within 48 hours
  • Conduct sessions professionally and in good faith
  • Provide guidance relevant to the skills listed on their profile
  • Accept that their reputation score may be affected by mentee reviews

5. Portfolio & Verified Data

Portfolio data is generated from verified system records. Members may not request the addition of false or unverified entries to their portfolio. All project contributions, workshop attendance, and CTF results are validated by administrators before being reflected in a member's profile.

6. Account Termination

CyberTitans administrators reserve the right to suspend or permanently terminate any account that violates these terms, engages in misconduct, or is found to be inactive for more than two consecutive semesters without notice. Members may also voluntarily request account deletion at any time.

7. Changes to Terms

These terms may be updated at any time by club administrators. Continued use of the platform after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised terms. Members will be notified of significant changes via the platform.
